18. buat 2 percakapan dalam bahasa Inggris menggunakan kalimat selamat harapan dan doa​


Kalimat harapan:

Wulan: Where have you been? I have not seen you for a week?

Anggoro : Well, I have been sick since Sunday

Wulan : What is wrong?

Anggoro : I have had a terrible cold all week.

Wulan : Oh, I am sorry to hear that. I hope you feel better soon.

Anggoro : Thanks, Wulan.

Doa:

Gina : Hi, Jean. How are you doing?

Jean : Bad.

Gina : What's wrong?

Jean : My father got sick. He has to stay in the hospital for a few days to get medical care.

Gina : What happened to him?

Jean : He got cancer.

Gina : I'm sorry to hear that. But you still go to school?

Jean : Yea, my mom told me that I have to go to school. She is in the hospital to accompany my dad.

Gina : I hope your dad will get better soon.

Jean : Yea, I hope so
