2. Cara membuat mie goreng dalam bahasa inggris beserta terjemahannya


Jawaban:

Material:

500 grams of yellow noodles, wash thoroughly, drain

1 bunch of mustard greens, wash thoroughly, cut

1 chicken egg, for scrambled

6 beef meatballs, thinly sliced

1 tomato, divide by 8

1 item leek, thinly sliced

4 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon of oyster sauce

1/2 tsp chicken powder broth

Salt to taste

Pepper to taste

Softened seasoning:

3 cloves of shallot

2 cloves of garlic

How to make:

Saute ground spices until fragrant.

Add the mustard greens, tomatoes, meatballs and scrambled eggs. Stir until the mustard greens wilt.

Add noodles, chicken broth, salt, pepper, oyster sauce and soy sauce. Stir until all ingredients are even.

Add leeks when the noodles are almost cooked. Stir well.

Lift and serve while warm.

23. cara membuat nasi goreng dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya?


Jawaban:

Ingredients:

2 cloves of Garlic

2 onions cut into small pieces

1 table spoon of vegetable oil

a plate of rice

some salt

Steps how to make it

First, pound garlic and 2 pinches of salt.

Second, heat vegetable oil on a frying pan.

Third, put the pounded garlic and salt, and onions into the hot vegetable oil. Fry them a while.

Fourth, put one plate of rice. Fry and mix them around 3 menit.

Fifth, add two pinches of salt. Continue frying until the rice is hot enough.

Finally, put the fried rice on a plate. Serve with a sunny-side up.
