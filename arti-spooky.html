PEMBAHASAN

Dalam menyusun suatu kata menjadi kalimat yang bermakna, maka kita harus pastikan apakah kalimatnya bentuk verbal atau nominal.

Ciri-ciri kalimat verbal

SubjectVerbComplement

Ciri-ciri kalimat nominal

SubjectTo be Complement (adjecetive, adverb of time, noun, adverb of place)

Pada soal diatas, kalimatnya bentuk simple past.

Rumus Simple Past (Verbal)

S + V2 + O

1). The security allowed us to enter the Guardiola Museum.

↪ Satpam mengijinkan kami masuk Museum Guardiola.

The security => SubjectAllowed => Verb-2 dari "allow"Us => ObjectTo enter the Guardiola Museum => Complement

2). We wore the spooky costumes during last hallowen party

↪ Kami memakai kostum yang seram delama pesta hallowen terakhir.

We => SubjectWore => Verb-2 dari "wear"The spooky costumes => ObjectDuring last hallowen party => Complement

Rumus Simple Past (Nominal)

S + To be (was/were) + Complement

3). I was very lucky to meet my old friend in this strange place.

↪ Saya sangat beruntung bertemu teman lama saya ditempat yang aneh ini.

I => SubjectWas => To beVery => AdverbLucky => AdjectiveTo meet my old friend in this strange place => ComplementLearn more about

28. Asking certaintycrespore do you believe that the house is spooky?


Answer:

Here are some expressions that you can use for asking certainty:

- Are you sure?

- Are you certain?

- Is it confirmed?

Here are some expressions that you can use for giving certainty:

- Of course / Sure

- I’m sure

- I’ve no doubt about …

Here is the example dialogue:

A: Do you believe that the house is spooky?

B: Are you sure?

A: Of course. I have heard many stories about it.
