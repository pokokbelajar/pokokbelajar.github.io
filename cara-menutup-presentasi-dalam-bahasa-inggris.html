1. Caranya membuka presentasi, menutup, dan bergantian dengan presenter lainnya ketika tugas presentasi bahasa inggris bagaimana?


membuka presentasi (jika presentasi sendirian):

"Good morning/afternoon/evening everyone, my name is ...... and i would like to presentate my assignment."

menutup presentasi

"Okay friends/sir/ma'am, that's enough, any question? (jika tidak ada pertanyaan, lanjutkan) this is end of my presentation, i'm sorry if there's so many mistake."

membuka presentasi (jika berkelompok)

"Good morning/afternoon/evening everyone, we would like to presentate us assignment. My name is ..... and her/his name is (sambil menunjuk anggota kelompok yang lain .... and her/his name is (lalu teman sekelompok tadi menunjuk anggota kelompok yang lain)" , dst.
