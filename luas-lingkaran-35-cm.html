1. jika sebuah lingkaran 35 cm berapa luas lingkaran tersebut?


961,625 cm²

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Jari-jari = 35 : 2 = 17,5 cm

[tex] \sf \: l = \pi \: \times {r}^{2} \\ \\ \sf \: l = 3,14 \times 17,5\times 17,5 \\ \\ \sf \: l = \pink {961,625 cm²}[/tex]

r = d ÷ 2

r = 35 ÷ 2

r = 17,5

L = π × r²

L = 3,14 × 17,5²

L = 3,14 × (17,5 × 17,5)

L = 3,14 × 306,25

L = 961,625 cm²

= ( π × r × r) / 2

= ( 22/7 × 35 × 35 ) / 2

= ( 22 × 5 × 35 ) / 2

= ( 110 × 35 ) / 2

= 3850/ 2

= 1.925 Cm²
