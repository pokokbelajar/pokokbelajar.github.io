There was a group of frogs. They were traveling through the woods, and two of them fell into a deep pit.When the other frogs saw how deep the pit was, they told the two frogs that they were as good as dead. The two frogs ignored the comments and tried to jump up out of the pit with all their might. The other frogs kept telling them to stop, that they were as good as dead.Finally, one of the frogs took heed to what the other frogs were saying and gave up.

Kelas: SMP 8
Pelajaran: Bahasa Inggris
Kategori: Narative Text
Kata Kunci: Sentence, Narative, Simple Present.


Narative Text adalah cerita imaginatif yang bertujuan menghibur pembaca. 

Generic Structure dari Narrative Text: 

1. Orientation: siapa atau apa, kapan dan dimana peristiwa itu terjadi. 
2. Complication: Kapan dan Dimana cerita tersebut mulai berkembang.
3.  Resolution: Ketika masalah didalam cerita mulai terselesaikan. 
4. Evaluation: berisi penilaian/evaluasi terhadap jalannya cerita atau konflik. Sedangkan
5. Reorientation berisi penyimpulan isi akhir cerita.

After that, the friend on the tree came down and asked his friend that was on the ground, "Friend, what did the bear whisper into your ears?"
