18. tuliskan teks prosedur cara membuat mie instan​


Jawaban:

Alat & Bahan :

1. Satu bungkus mie instan (goreng/kuah)

2. Piring atau mangkuk 

3. Saringan jika membuat mie instan goreng                                                             

*Langkah-langkah untuk membuat mie instan : 

1. Siapkan panci untuk merebus mie instan

2. Masukan air ke dalam panci secukupnya

3. Nyalakan kompor lalu rebus air sampai mendidih

4. Buka bungkus mie instan dan pisahkan bumbu dengan mie, lalu buka bumbunya 

5. Setelah itu masukan mie kedalam panci yang sudah berisi air mendidih 

6. Tunggu mie selama 5 menit 

7. Setelah mie sudah matang, tuangkan mie ke dalam piring/mangkuk yang sudah kita siapkan terlebih                                                                       dahulu

8. Aduk mie agar merata dengan bumbu

9. Selesai mie siap disajikan/dimakan

HOW TO COOK THE CORRECT INSTANT Noodles

HEALTH INFO, TIPS

The Right Way to Cook Instant Noodles - Instant noodles are one of the most popular foods in the world, including in Indonesia. Instant noodles have a variety of flavors that shake the tongue because of their enjoyment, especially when served warm. Instant noodles were introduced in Indonesia in 1968, and two years after that the first instant noodle brand in Indonesia, namely indomie, was launched. The public demand for instant noodles is very much, so there are also more companies producing instant noodles, and the most dominating is PT. Indofood Sukese Makmur.

Well, if you are a fan of instant noodles, then you must know that instant noodles cannot replace carbohydrate needs in your body, even though instant noodles contain carbohydrates. What you need to know is that each strand of instant noodles is coated with a waxy substance, which is a substance that keeps the noodles from sticking to one another. Wax is a substance that is difficult for the body to digest, because it takes the body 3 days to digest the wax.

￼

For this reason, if you like to eat instant noodles, you should limit your consumption of instant noodles at least once in 3 days so that the instant noodle content in your body that you previously consumed is gone. Because if you consume instant noodles with a short time interval, then you have the potential to get cancer because of the buildup of wax in the body. Wax is also low in fiber, so it can make it hard for you to have a bowel movement.

For those of you who want to avoid the risk of disease due to instant noodles, we will provide information on how to cook instant noodles properly so that you avoid various diseases.

1. Fried noodles

How to cook fried instant noodles is as follows:

a. Boil the noodles with boiling water.

b. Once they are fluffy enough, drain the noodles for a few minutes.

c. After draining, rinse the noodles with new hot water (not hot water from cooking the noodles).

d. Mix with the spices.

e. Enjoy your favorite fried noodles.

2. Boiled noodles

How to cook boiled instant noodles are as follows:

a. Boil the noodles with boiling water.

b. After it is cooked enough, drain the noodles for a few minutes and discard the water from the noodle stew.

MIE INSTAN
Mie instan memiliki rasa/khas yang berbeda.Ada yang dapat dinikmati pada suasana dingin/panas.

LANGKAH-LANGKAH MEMBUAT MIE INSTAN:
Bahan:
1) Mie instan
2) Air

Alat:
1) Wajan
2) Samsi

Siapkan wajan yang sudah terisi air,lalu nyalakan apinya.Tunggu hingga mendidih,setelah sudah mendidih tuangkan mie instan.Sambil menunggu mie instan matang,tuangkan bumbu di wadah,lalu lihat mie instan apakah sudah mendidih?kalau sudah angkat mie tersebut.Dan matikan apinya,tuangkan mie kedalam bumbu,aduk hingga merata.Mie instan pun siap disajikan☺

Mie instan dibuat dengan sangat praktis dan mudah silahkan mencoba,terima kasih.

Alat-alat yang harus disediakan adalah sebagai berikut.


   Panci.

   Penyaringan.

   Mangkok.

   Sendok


       Bahan-bahan yang harus disediakan adalah sebagai berikut.


   Mie instan.

   Air panas.


       Adapun langkah-langkahnya adalah sebagai berikut.


   Pertama, masukkan air panas kedalam panci lalu nyalakan kompor. Kemudian, buka kemasan mie instan lalu rebus mie.

   Kedua, tunggu kira-kira 3 menit supaya matang, setelah matang tiriskan mie menggunakan penyaringan dan siram mie menggunakan air panas.

   Ketiga, campurkan bumbu mie instan ke dalam mangkok. Lalu, tuangkan air panas ke dalam mangkok.

   Keempat, tuangkan mie ke dalam mangkok yang telah diberi bumbu.

   Setelah itu, mie instan pun bisa dinikmati.
