grab 1 pack of an instant noodle and a bowl. open the pack and Add the seasoning to the bowl, after that prepared a pan, Bring 2 ½ cups of water to a boil in a large saucepan over high heat, boil it until the water bubbles, then turn off the stove, and pour ½ of the water gently to the bowl and mix it well, after that turn on again the stove and add the instant noodle to the pan, boil it until it cooked well, then take a filter then strain the noodles and place in a bowl , do not forget to give some garnish on the top of the noodle, then serve it.
