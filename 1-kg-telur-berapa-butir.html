1. Jika 1 kg telur berisi 16 butir berapa kg kah 6 butir telur???


1 kg ------> 16 butir

x kg-------> 6 butir

[tex]\frac{1}{x} = \frac{16}{6} \\ x = \frac{6}{16} \\ x = \frac{3}{8} = 0.375kg[/tex]

6 butir
= 6/16 × 1
= 3/8 × 1
= 0,375 kg
