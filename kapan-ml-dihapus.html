Penjelasan:

Diketahui:
Volume = 5.000 mL
V = 5 L
Konsentrasi = 0,02 M
M = 0,02 M
Nilai Ar molekul KOH:
KOH, Ar:
--> K = 39 g/mol
--> O = 16 g/mol
--> H = 1 g/mol

Dicari: massa (m)
Cari dulu jumlah mol (n)
n = M×V
n = 0,02×5
n = 0,1 mol

Lalu cari massa (m)
m = n×(K+O+H)
m = 0,1×(39+16+1)
m = 0,1×56
m = 5,6 gram (b)
