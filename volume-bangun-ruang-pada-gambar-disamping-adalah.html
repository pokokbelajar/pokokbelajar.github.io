28. perhatiksn gambar di samping. volume bangun ruang tersebut


Mapel : Matematika
Bab : volume & luas pemukaan bangun ruang

Volume bangun gabungan tersebut = vol. prisma segitiga + vol. balok

Volume prisma segitiga = 1/2 x alas x tinggi x tinggi
                                      = 1/2 x 10 x (20 - 12) x 18
                                      = 1/2 x 10 x 8 x 18
                                      = 10 x 8 x 9
                                      = 10 x 72
                                      = 720 cm²

Volume balok = panjang x lebar x tinggi
                       = 18 x 10 x 12
                       = 216 x 10
                       = 2.160 cm²

Volume gabungan = 720 + 2.160
                              = 2.880 cm²

Semoga membantu
