Rabbits are small mammals found in several parts of the world. Rabbits are similar to rodents in that they have incisor teeth that continually grow.

Rabbits are herbivores (plant eating) mammals. The eat vegetables, tree bark and herbs. A rabbit’s diet is very important. The wrong food can kill a rabbit.

Rabbits have a long ears, which can be more than 10 cm long, and their ears are probably an adaptation for detecting predators. They have large, powerful hind legs. The two front paws have 5 toes, the extra called the dewclaw. The hind feet have 4 toes.

Their size can range anywhere from 20 cm in length and 0.4 kg in weight to 50 cm and more than 2 kg. The fur is most commonly long and soft, with colors such as shades of brown, gray, and buff.

Rabbit’s tail is a little plume of brownish fur, and rabbits can see nearly 360 degrees, with a small blind spot at the bridge of the nose.

24. deskripsi kelinci dalam bahasa inggris


Writing Descriptive Text

Rabbit

Rabbit is a small sized mammal with thick fur and long ears. Their ears are the most noticeable characteristic from this animal.

With those ears they could hear better and recognize voices of their predators. Rabbits have powerful legs, they move by hopping. Some species of rabbit can reach speeds up to 70 kilometers per hour. While for young rabbits, they tend to walk instead of hop.

[Terjemahan]

Kelinci

Kelinci adalah mamalia berukuran kecil yang memiliki bulu tebal dan telinga panjang. Telinga mereka adalah ciri-ciri yang paling mudah dikenali dari binatang ini.

Dengan kedua telinga tersebut, kelinci dapat mendengar lebih baik dan mengenali suara-suara dari para pemangsa mereka. Kelinci memiliki kaki yang kuat, mereka berpindah tempat dengan melompat. Beberapa spesies dari kelinci dapat mencapai kecepatan hingga 70 kilometer per jam. Sementara untuk kelinci muda, biasanya mereka cenderung berjalan daripada melompat.

Pembahasan

Deskripsi tentang kelinci di atas masuk dalam kategori descriptive text. Descriptive text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memberikan informasi mengenai suatu tempat, orang, binatang, dll. Pada descriptive text sifatnya lebih mengarah kepada penggambaran, pengungkapan secara detil dan penjelasan.

Generic structure dari descriptive text adalah:

Identification => Pendahuluan, yakni gambaran umum suatu topikDescription => Ciri-ciri tentang topik yang dibahas, sifat-sifat yang dimiliki, tampilan khas, dll.

This order is divided into two families, namely Ochtonidae (a type of pika who is good at whistling) and Leporidae (including rabbits and hares). The origin of the word rabbit comes from the Dutch language, namely konijntje which means "bunny rabbit". This shows that the people of the Archipelago began to recognize rabbits during the colonial period, even though on the island of Sumatra there was one native species of the Sumatran rabbit (Nesolagus netscheri) which was only discovered in 1972. Currently a number of types of rabbits for pets and meat animals. Several types of rabbits as meat animals are also used as pets.
