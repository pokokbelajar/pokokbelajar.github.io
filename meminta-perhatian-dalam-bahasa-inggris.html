25. contoh dialog bahasa inggris tentang meminta perhatian dan meminta pendapat !


kelas : VIII
pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : expressions
kata kunci : dialog, asking for attention, asking for opinion

pembahasan:
asking for attention dialog (dialog meminta perhatian)
you : excuse me. Where can I find a ruler like this?
the shopkeeper : let me see. Oh. You can find it at the corner.
you : thank you.
the shopkeeper : you're welcome.

asking for opinion dialog (dialog meminta pendapat)
you : Look at this! what do you think about my new dress?
your friend : It looks beautiful. But, I think it is too small for you.
you : Yes, I think so. I should bring it back to the shop.
your friend : I think it's a good idea.

asking for attention expression:
- excuse me ..
- look at this!

asking for opinion expression:
- what do you think about ...?

giving opinion expression:
- I think it is too small for you.
- I think it's a good idea.

dialog tentang meminta perhatian dan meminta pendapat
you : Look! I find something! (asking for attention)
your friend : what is it? it looks like a seed.
you : I am not sure. what do you think about this? (asking opinion)
your friend : I think it is a seed. where do you find it? (giving opinion)
you : I found it at our school backyard.
your friend : Do you believe that a seed? (asking opinion)
you : No, i don't. I think it is a stone. (giving opinion)
your friend : Hmm..let's ask our teacher.
you : okay.
